Discover Taraz

Taraz, one of the oldest cities in Kazakhstan, is a treasure trove of history and culture. Known for its ancient heritage, Taraz boasts stunning archaeological sites, traditional bazaars, and a vibrant local culture. With over 2,000 years of history, it is a city where the past and present blend seamlessly.
